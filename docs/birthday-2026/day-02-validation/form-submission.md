## Short Description

For Day 2 I built Sprint Room — a multiplayer workspace where humans and Sprint AI plan together in one shared room. Presence, collaborative notes, clarifications, and AI plan packets sync live over PartyKit/Yjs, with Kiro specs and steering driving the build.

## How Kiro Was Used

I used Kiro’s spec workflow (requirements → design → tasks) for Sprint Room, plus steering files (`product`, `tech`, `ai-teammate`). Day 2’s deliverable is the live multiplayer room: humans join with a display name, Sprint AI stays in presence, and Clarify/Plan actions update the shared sprint packet for everyone.
